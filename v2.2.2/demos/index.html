About Us

Who We Are

WE ARE areleo

In mid-2017, Mikel Yaw, Annie Bahwani and assisted by 6 passionated youngsters, tried to reinvented the online communication platform by making a realtime communication application for mobile devices and smart glasses for industrial manufactures where user able to maintain & fix its equipment & machine remotely, fast and inexpensive.

Through its areleo Visual Assistance platform, areleo wants to satisfy the manufactures and engineers with easy ways to video conference, chat, multimedia collaboration and augmented reality feature into one single epicenter.

MIKEL YAW

Founder & CEO

ANNIE BAHWANI

Co-Founder & CFO

OUR VISION

To provide solution to manage the complexity of collaboration and communication obstacles through a seamless communication technology in industrial, to achieve result faster, simpler and cost effective.

OUR MISSION

We believe in our technology, our passion and our product can deliver best solution for industrial maintenance and communication issues. With our strong empowered and innovative team, we make communication and collaboration effortless.
